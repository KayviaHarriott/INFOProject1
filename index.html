Biography

Elbert Hubbard once said, "If you want anything done, ask a busy man to do it." Hopefully, if it is God's will, I will be able to work alongside some of the greatest minds in programming and also produce illustrations for companies such as Disney, Nickelodeon and Cartoon Network.
